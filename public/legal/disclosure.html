General Disclosure for Our Website & Games

Last updated July 16, 2026 · Applies to aariasblueelephant.org — every page of the website and all games and interactive activities

Who we are

Aaria's Blue Elephant ("ABE," "we") is a nonprofit initiative building a more inclusive world for children, including children with autism and other special needs. Our website and games are built by volunteers and offered free of charge.

Educational purpose only

Our games are designed for learning through play — practicing everyday skills, social understanding, and personal-safety awareness. They are not medical, psychological, therapeutic, legal, or emergency services, and they are not a substitute for professional evaluation, therapy (including ABA or counseling), medical advice, or the judgment of a child's caregivers, educators, and clinicians.

A grown-up should stay involved

  • These games are intended to be played with adult awareness and involvement. Some activities ask an adult to review content before a child plays it, and adults can re-lock any section at any time.
  • Safety-education content (for example, recognizing bullying and telling a trusted adult) is simplified for young learners. Please talk with your child about what they play — the conversation is the most important part.
  • Every child is different. Caregivers know best whether a topic, pace, or activity suits their child.

If a child needs help right now

  • In an emergency, call 911.
  • Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline (24/7, call or text): 1-800-422-4453.
  • Crisis support (call or text): 988.

These services are independent of ABE; we list them for convenience and do not operate them.

Privacy — what our games store

  • Our games run entirely in your browser. Progress, stickers, saved creations, and adult sign-offs are stored only on your own device (browser local storage).
  • We do not require accounts for games, and the games themselves do not collect, transmit, or sell personal information about you or your child.
  • Clearing your browser data will erase game progress and any adult sign-offs on that device.

No warranties; limitation of liability

The games are provided "as is," without warranties of any kind, express or implied. While we test our games with care, we cannot guarantee they are error-free or uninterrupted. To the fullest extent permitted by law, ABE, its volunteers, and contributors are not liable for any damages arising from the use of, or inability to use, these games. Use of the games is at the user's discretion and risk, under the supervision of a responsible adult.

Content and characters

All characters and scenarios are fictional and taught by role (for example, "the teacher," "a firefighter") rather than by personal name. Any resemblance to real people is coincidental. Game content may change as we improve it, including in response to clinical review and caregiver feedback.

AI-assisted content — it may not always be ideal

Much of the content in our games and across this website — spoken lines, learning activities, practice scripts (including grown-up-and-child role-play prompts), stories, and translations — is created with the help of AI and reviewed by volunteers who are parents, not licensed clinicians. Despite our care, some wording, teaching approach, or activity may not be ideal for every child, and some content may be published before a clinical professional has reviewed it. Please treat the games as a friendly practice companion, not professional guidance, and adapt anything that does not suit your child. If you notice anything that seems off, confusing, or unsuitable — however small — please tell us at hi@aariasblueelephant.org. We read every message and fix things gratefully and quickly.

Gran parte del contenido de nuestros juegos y de este sitio web — frases habladas, actividades, guiones de práctica (incluidos los de juego de roles entre adulto y niño), historias y traducciones — se crea con ayuda de inteligencia artificial y lo revisan voluntarios que son padres, no clínicos con licencia. A pesar de nuestro cuidado, alguna frase, enfoque o actividad podría no ser ideal para cada niño, y parte del contenido puede publicarse antes de una revisión clínica profesional. Usa los juegos como un compañero de práctica amistoso, no como guía profesional, y adapta lo que no le convenga a tu hijo. Si notas algo raro, confuso o inadecuado — por pequeño que sea — escríbenos a hi@aariasblueelephant.org. Leemos cada mensaje y lo corregimos con gratitud y rapidez.

Spanish translation / Traducción al español

The Spanish version of our website and games was produced entirely using AI translation. This applies to every Spanish text on aariasblueelephant.org — pages, menus, resources, and games alike. The creators are not Spanish speakers, and while we have tried to keep the translations warm, accurate, and age-appropriate, they may contain mistakes. The English text remains the authoritative version — in particular for legal pages and for safety-education content, which undergoes clinical review in English. If you find an error or awkward phrasing, please tell us; we will fix it gratefully.

La versión en español de nuestro sitio web y de nuestros juegos fue creada completamente con inteligencia artificial. Esto aplica a todo texto en español de aariasblueelephant.org — páginas, menús, recursos y juegos por igual. Los creadores no hablan español y, aunque intentamos que las traducciones sean cálidas, correctas y apropiadas para cada edad, pueden contener errores. El texto en inglés es la versión oficial — especialmente las páginas legales y el contenido de educación sobre seguridad, que pasa por revisión clínica en inglés. Si encuentras un error o una frase extraña, por favor escríbenos; lo corregiremos con mucho gusto.
